<p style="text-align: center;" markdown>**Each player chooses one option:** <br>Draw 4 cards either from your discard pile or your deck. Then Remove any number of [Spell](../spells/index.md) or [Ability](../abilities/index.md) cards from your hand. For every two cards removed  this way, you can perform **Search(2)** the [Spell](../spells/index.md) deck or the [Ability](../abilities/index.md) deck. After that, discard all cards from hand and draw up to your :hand_limit:.<br><br>— OR —<br><br>Leave and gain nothing.</p>
