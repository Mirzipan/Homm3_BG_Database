<p style="text-align: center;" markdown>When revealed, all players roll 2 [Resource dice](../keywords/dice.md#resource-die). The highest roller chooses an unused [Faction](../towns/index.md). The random [Town](../towns/index.md) is defended by [Units](../units/index.md) from that [Faction](../towns/index.md). They have a "Pack" of :bronze_tier:, two "Packs" of :silver_tier:, and two "Fews" of :golden: [Units](../units/index.md). The :bronze_tier: [Unit](../units/index.md) is chosen by the player who controls the [Units](../units/index.md) during that Combat.<br><br>When flagged, increases :gold: income by 10. The first player to flag the [Town](../towns/index.md) also gains its income immediately.</p>
